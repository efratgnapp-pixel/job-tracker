Import LinkedIn Connections

How to export your LinkedIn connections:

  1. Go to LinkedIn → click Me → Settings & Privacy
  2. Open Data privacy in the left menu
  3. Click "Get a copy of your data"
  4. Select Connections and click Request archive
  5. LinkedIn emails you a download link — usually within minutes
  6. Download the archive and upload the Connections.csv file below
